The problem

Kigali moves fast.
Logistics doesn't.

Rwanda's economy is accelerating. E-commerce is growing 30%+ annually. Yet the delivery infrastructure hasn't caught up — it's fragmented, opaque, and impossible to scale.

Businesses rely on WhatsApp, phone calls, and guesswork to manage deliveries that should be automated, visible, and predictable.

📍

Zero real-time visibility

Customers have no idea where their package is. Businesses can't track their riders. Every delivery is a black box.

📱

WhatsApp is not a dispatch system

No data, no decisions

Business owners can't see their delivery performance, rider efficiency, or revenue trends. Flying blind is expensive.

Fossil fuel dependency

Petrol-powered delivery fleets face volatile fuel costs and growing environmental pressure in a green-focused Rwanda.
